<h1> Widowmaker </h1> <div class="back_button" onclick="back()"><<<</div>
<p>
Widowmaker is a sound choice for skilled players to exercise their dominance. She excels at long range combat and does the most damage per shot in the game.
Good Widowmakers will take advantage of the scope by frequently zooming in and out. To play her to the highest level, players will have to
become accurate at high sensitivity gameplay. What sets Widowmaker over the top is her ultimate ability. Gaining sight and giving teammates knowledge of opponents
whereabouts will prove to be the most useful skill in the game.
</p>

<h2>Counterplay</h2><br>
<ul>
<li> High-mobility</li>
<li> Keep your team inside.</li>
<li> Shields.</li>
<li> Close combat. </li>
</ul>
